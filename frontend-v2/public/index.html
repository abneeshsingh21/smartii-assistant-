Data Collection

SMARTII Assistant processes voice commands locally on your device. We collect:

  • Voice transcriptions (processed locally)
  • User preferences and settings (stored in browser)
  • Conversation history (stored locally)

Data Usage

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide AI assistance and answer queries
  • Improve response accuracy
  • Personalize your experience
  • Remember your preferences across sessions

All data is stored locally on your device using:
